The Reality of B2B Receivables
Your invoices aren't getting paid. Here's why.

It's rarely about ability to pay. It's about process failures, bureaucratic friction, and the invoices that get buried when nobody follows up.

The Growth Penalty

You're scaling, but cash flow can't keep up. Revenue grows on paper while receivables pile up. Growth becomes a liability instead of an asset.

The Bureaucratic Loop

Your invoice sits in someone's inbox, waiting for an approval that's stuck in a queue. Not disputed — just lost in the machine. Without persistent follow-up, it stays there.

The Resource Dilemma

Hiring a full-time AR specialist is expensive. Not hiring one means your sales team — or you — are doing collections calls instead of closing deals.

The Relationship Trap

You don't want to damage customer relationships by chasing payments aggressively. So you wait. And wait. Meanwhile, your invoices get buried under everyone else's.
